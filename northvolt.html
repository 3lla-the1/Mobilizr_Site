Have you been affected by the Northvolt layoffs?

Are you an international professional? Mobilizr connects you directly with employers across Sweden. Are you an employer? Access top-tier talent ready to start now! Mobilizr will handle all the administration for the work permit for free!

Frequently Asked Questions by Candidates

You’re eligible if you:

  • Previously worked at Northvolt
  • Are registered with Arbetsförmedlingen
  • Hold a valid residence permit

This program is designed for international professionals affected by Northvolt layoffs. Apply Now

After signing up, we match your skills and experience with employers in our network of over 500 companies across Sweden. Once matched, employers contact you directly. Be ready to respond and discuss next steps, such as interviews or job offers.

Get Started

If hired through Mobilizr, we streamline the work permit process by:

  • Coordinating offer letters
  • Guiding your employer through compliance
  • Checking and preparing documents
  • Walking you through the application submission

You cover the Migration Agency’s fees (SEK 2,200 for standard permits/extensions, SEK 1,500–2,200 for family members). We handle the non-legal steps to ensure a smooth process. Note: We do not act as legal representatives.

Join Now

If you’re a permit holder laid off from Northvolt:

  • You have 3 months from your termination date to find a new job and submit a new work permit application.
  • Within the first 24 months of your permit, you need a new permit to start with a new employer.
  • After 24 months, if you stay in the same industry and role type, you may not need a new permit, but your employer must follow reporting and compliance procedures.
  • You can only extend a permit if it’s still valid. If it expires during your job search without a new offer, you cannot extend it.

Mobilizr simplifies the process to help you meet these requirements. Apply Today

Frequently Asked Questions for Employers

Mobilizr connects you with pre-vetted, skilled professionals from Northvolt, ready to contribute from day one. Our network ensures you access top-tier talent quickly, saving time and resources in your hiring process.

Start Hiring

After signing up, you’ll receive profiles of candidates matched to your job requirements. You can contact candidates directly to arrange interviews and make offers. Mobilizr supports you throughout, ensuring a smooth hiring experience.

Get Started

We guide you through the work permit process for hired candidates, including:

  • Coordinating compliant offer letters
  • Ensuring documentation meets Migrationsverket’s requirements
  • Preparing and checking application materials
  • Providing step-by-step guidance for submission

Candidates cover the Migration Agency’s fees (SEK 2,200 for permits/extensions). We handle non-legal steps to simplify compliance. Hire Now

As an employer, you must:

  • Provide a compliant job offer meeting Migrationsverket’s standards
  • Follow reporting and compliance procedures, especially if hiring candidates after their first 24 months of permit
  • Support candidates with timely documentation for permit applications
