Where It Began

In fall 2024, we met through the Ideate program, a brilliant mix of students from KTH, Stockholm School of Economics, Karolinska Institutet, and GIH. The three of us aligned quickly; unified on pace, mindset, and the desire to build something real. We dove into Sweden’s healthcare sector, talking to companies, hospitals, and researchers. We asked way too many questions, listened, and fell down more than a few rabbit holes—not yet focused on global mobility, but driven to understand the bigger picture.

The Spark

We had a plenty of ideas. Solving scheduling for hospitals. A smart language system to help international doctors hit the ground running. A unified medical record for vaccinations (because yes, it’s still scattered across providers in Sweden). Even preventive health tech for pets. But the more people we talked to, the more one pattern kept showing up. Whether it was healthcare, tech, public agencies, or startups, we kept hearing the same thing: “We can’t find the talent we need.”

But talent was not lacking, it was locked. The real challenge was access; systems built to move bright minds were slow, fragmented, and full of friction. That sparked the question: What if we could change that?

Redefining Mobility

How can we be the change we wish to see in the world? How do we fuel innovation? The more we iterate and listen, the clearer it becomes: It starts with people. If we want progress, we must trust in people’s capacity to lead it.

We are butilding Mobilizr because we believe belonging transcends geography, language, or a passport — it’s the opportunity to contribute and be part of something greater than oneself. As dreamers and founders, we are united by a shared belief in movement.

A World in Motion

We see it clearly: the power in people. Our mission is to streamline global mobility, and Mobilizr is the engine that empowers companies to welcome exceptional talent.
